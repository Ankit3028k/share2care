About Share2care

Empowering Elders
Through Time Sharing & Supportive Connections

Share2Care Foundation is a registered non-profit initiative focused on creating a vibrant social platform for elderly citizens. Through our unique “Time Sharing” concept, we enable meaningful connections among seniors based on shared interests and life experiences. We also offer free support across healthcare, education, legal aid, emotional well-being, and skill development—especially for senior citizens, women, and underserved communities.

  • Facilitate safe, interest-based meetups for senior citizens
  • Promote emotional and mental well-being through engagement
  • Offer educational, legal, and health guidance free of cost
  • Enable profile creation with verified KYC for trust and safety
